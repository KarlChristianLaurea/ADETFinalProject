UlamAlam

A Filipino Recipe Recommendation System Aligned with Ingredient Availability

About

INTRODUCTION

Purpose of Document

UlamAlam is a web-based application that aims to help students who are having a hard time in thinking about what dish they are going to cook in their dormitories. This application would help recommend Filipino dishes from the selected ingredients options and what recipe can be used for cooking. This would be beneficial for those students who lack knowledge of cooking, and those students who are having a hard time thinking what meal they are going to eat. Having this application would provide student knowledge at the same time enhance their cooking skills.

Project Scope

UlamAlam is a web-based application aimed at streamlining meal planning and enhancing cooking skills for students residing in dormitories by offering personalized Filipino dish suggestions based on the ingredients they have available. This initiative seeks to resolve the common issue of meal indecision and acts as an educational resource that introduces a variety of Filipino cuisines and culinary methods. The project's objectives include crafting a user-friendly interface for ingredient selection, building a comprehensive database of Filipino recipes with detailed cooking instructions and nutritional facts, updating ingredient availability in real time to ensure accurate dish recommendations, and providing educational content about the cultural significance and history of each dish to promote a deeper appreciation for Filipino cuisine.

Limitations

Several limitations may impact the recipe suggestion application. Inaccurate ingredient availability data can lead to incorrect recommendations. A limited collection of Filipino recipes might result in repetitive suggestions. Users, especially students, may become overly dependent on the app, hindering their cooking skills development. Technical issues like glitches, crashes, or server outages can affect usability. Privacy and data security concerns may arise if user data is mishandled. Unappetizing recipe suggestions can reduce user interaction. Performance issues, such as slow response times during high usage, can impact system efficiency.
